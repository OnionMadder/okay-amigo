OkayAmigo Internal Mail
One of the main benefits of the OkayAmigo system is the ability to send emails to members that you are interested in getting to know. OkayAmigo email is completely internal. What this means is that instead of simply forwarding your initial contact to your personal email address, every email sent is kept within the security and privacy of OkayAmigo system. You have full control over who you email, who emails you, and how your contact with them evolves from there. You can remain anonymous for as long as you like and you never have to divulge any personal information, email or otherwise, until you are ready. You can also block emails from specific members if they act like idiots. This ensures your security and privacy at all times and provides the safest of environments to meet people.
